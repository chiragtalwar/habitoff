Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2024

At Habito ("we", "our", or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Habit tracking data (habit names, completion dates, streaks)
  • Basic usage statistics to improve the extension
  • Authentication information when you sign in

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the habit tracking service
  • Sync your habits across devices
  • Improve and optimize the extension
  • Send important updates about the service

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for data storage, which maintains high security standards. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
