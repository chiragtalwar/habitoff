Refund Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2024

Thank you for choosing Habito. We want to ensure your satisfaction with our service. This Refund Policy outlines our procedures and conditions for refunds.

Subscription Refunds

Our refund policy is as follows:

  • You may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial subscription purchase
  • Refund requests must be submitted through our support email
  • Refunds will be processed through your original payment method
  • Processing time for refunds may take 5-10 business days

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered in the following cases:

  • Technical issues preventing the use of premium features
  • Accidental or duplicate purchases
  • Service unavailability during the subscription period
  • Dissatisfaction with the service within the first 7 days

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • After the 7-day refund period has expired
  • For partial months of service
  • If terms of service have been violated
  • For subscriptions canceled after renewal
